SPECTRAL SESSIONS

Live collaborative art creation streamed every Sunday at 2PM EST. Multiple artists draw simultaneously while viewers vote on attributes — color, subject, mood. At the end of each session, a hue-shifting color effect transforms all artwork live. A weekly demonstration of the Spectral Art movement.

The Show

Artists share their screens and create original work over 4 hours while we discuss Spectral Art technique, color theory, and XRPL provenance. The audience shapes the art through live voting.

S.A.F.E.

Spectral Art Foundation Enterprises · 501(c)(3)

SAFE empowers artists through grants, workshops, exhibitions, and zero-barrier access to patented Spectral Frame technology. Physical artwork engineered to come alive through LED hue-cycling. XRPL-verified provenance.
